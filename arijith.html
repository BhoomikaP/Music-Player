Arijith Singh

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian singer.He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali, but has also performed in various other Indian languages. Singh is regarded as one of the most versatile and successful singers in the history of Indian music and Hindi cinema.He made his career breakthrough after he participated in the contemporary reality show, Fame Gurukul in 2005 . He has delivered a lot of hits since then.
