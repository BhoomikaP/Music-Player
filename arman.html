Armaan Malik

Armaan Malik (born 22 July 1995) is an Indian singer and an actor. He mainly sings in Hindi films, but has also sung in various other Indian languages. He also sang the title track of the Pakistani film "Jannan". He is the brother of composer Amaal Mallik previously represented by Universal Music India, and now signed on by T-Series. Armaan is often regarded by many notable artists as one of the best upcoming male singers of Bollywood due to his soothing voice.
